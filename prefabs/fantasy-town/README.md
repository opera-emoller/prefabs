# Fantasy Town

Cute Building sprites for your next medieval or fantasy game. Great for city builders, RTS games, or your virtual tabletop.

Based on the Fantasy Town Asset Bundle
Genre: Medieval fantasy buildings.
Style: Colourful, cartoony, 3D rendered to 2D sprites.
Specific Elements: Front, Top Down, Side and Isometric views of multiple houses, guard towers, market stalls, trees, windmills and more.
