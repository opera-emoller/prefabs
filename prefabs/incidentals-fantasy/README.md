# Incidentals - Fantasy

A mixture of icons and images in color, and black and white.

Based on the Incidentals Asset Bundle.

- **Genre:** UI and Game Icons
- **Style:** Rendered, colorful, black and white.
- **Sprites:** 130+ including books, containers, gadgets, reward items, magic
  items and resources — each in glossy rendered color (plain and outlined) plus
  flat-black and flat-white silhouette variants.
