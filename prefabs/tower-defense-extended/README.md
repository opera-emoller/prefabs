# Tower Defense - Extended

Towers of every kind that slot perfectly into the Towers vs Monsters GameMaker game Template Project.

Based on the Tower Defense Extended Asset Bundle
Genre: Tower Defence Sprites
Style: Hand drawn, cartoon.
Sprites: 10 new towers with 12 degrees of rotation
VFX: Yes
Specific Elements: 10 new tower types with 12 degrees of rotation. New towers are Cannon, Explosive, Flamer, Freeze, Heal, Holy, Magic, Poison, Rail and Slow. All towers come with new VFX sprites and sprite sheets. 

Note: Goes great with the Towers vs Monsters GameMaker game Template Project.
