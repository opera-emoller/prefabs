# Iconic Animals

Images of animals in a variety of styles from painted to pixel art that are perfect for sorting and zoo keeper games.

Based on the Iconic Animals Asset bundle
Genre: Sprite icons
Style: Pixel art, cartoon, outline and non-outline.
Sprites: 50+ Animal face sprites
Specific Elements: Axolotl, Badger, Bat, Panda Bear, Polar Bear, Cartoon Bear, Capybara, 3 Cats, Chick, Chicken, 3 Cows, Crocodile, 2 Deer, 4 Dogs, Duck, Octopus, Fox, 3 Frogs, Owl, Giraffe, Goat, Hamster, Hedgehog, Horse, Lion, Monkey, Mouse, 2 Otters, Owl, Penguin, Pig, 3 Rabbits, Snake, 2 Squirrels, Tiger and Zebra. All images in both cartoon and pixel styles. All images with a black stroked outline and a non-stroked version.
