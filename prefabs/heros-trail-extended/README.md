# Hero's Trail - Extended

Extra assets for the Hero's Trail Template Project that also stand alone great as an addition to your next game.

Based on the Hero's Trail Extended Asset Bundle
Genre: Action and Exploration GameMaker
Style: High resolution pixel art, shaded, cartoony.
Characters: 1 new Slime enemy with attack and move up, attack and move side, idle and death animations.
Objects: 15 Floor sprites, 8 doors, 6 switches, 2 treasure chests, 20+ wall clutter sprites, and more.
Animation: Yes
Tilesets: Yes
VFX: Yes
